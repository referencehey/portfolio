Project Inquiry & Onboarding Protocol

To maintain a high standard of development and ensure project compatibility, please provide the following details in your initial outreach.

Provide a high-level overview of the game concept, target audience, and current development stage. I am looking for projects with a clear vision and defined core loop.

Define the specific systems required (e.g., Azul-driven UI architecture, complex backend data-routing, or CI/CD pipeline setup). Mention if you have existing technical constraints or specific frameworks in use.

Specify if this is a modular commission (a single system), a long-term departmental lead role, or full-stack support. Clarity on deliverables prevents scope creep and ensures accurate quoting.

State your established budget range for the project. I prioritize inquiries that offer transparent, milestone-based compensation (USD or Robux) aligned with industry standards for senior engineering.

Attach or link your Game Design Document (GDD), technical specifications, or wireframes. Projects with pre-production documentation are prioritized for review.

Include your primary timezone and preferred communication platform (Discord/Guilded). Please note your expected start date and mandatory hard deadlines.
